Oracle NetSuite ERP Description

Oracle NetSuite is the #1 cloud ERP solution in the world. It provides a centralized platform to manage all back-office processes, like accounting, eCommerce, and CRM. NetSuite has inbuilt business intelligence, reporting, and renders real-time visibility across your organization. It empowers the industries to reduce IT costs, unleash growth, and eliminate operational incompetence- allowing you to focus on revenue-generating decisions, rather than your back-end systems.
